About Siri Agro Foods Private Limited

Siri Agro Foods Private Limited is a dedicated agro-processing company specializing in the manufacturing of high-quality Soya Meal from premium soya bean seeds. Established with a commitment to quality and reliability, we operate a state-of-the-art processing facility with a capacity of 500 Metric Tons Per Day, supplying to animal feed manufacturers, poultry farms, aqua feed producers, and agro traders across South and West India.

Our manufacturing process ensures consistent protein content, optimal moisture levels, and superior nutritional value in every batch — making us a trusted supplier across multiple states.
